## Phase 2: Pricing Strategy

### Free Tier (User Acquisition)
- Unlimited invitation creation
- 3 free AI generations per month
- Basic templates
- Watermarked downloads
- **Goal:** Maximize signups, build email list

### Pro Tier - $9.99/month
- Unlimited AI generations
- No watermarks
- Premium templates
- Priority support
- Custom domains for invitation links
- **Your cost:** ~$2-3/month in AI generations (heavy user)
- **Margin:** 70-80%

### Pay-As-You-Go Credits
| Package | Price | Your Cost | Margin |
|---------|-------|-----------|--------|
| 5 generations | $2.99 | $0.35 | 88% |
| 15 generations | $6.99 | $1.05 | 85% |
| 50 generations | $14.99 | $3.50 | 77% |

### Print Orders (Prodigi)
- Markup: 30-50% on top of Prodigi base price
- Example: Prodigi charges $15 for 25 cards → You charge $22-25
- Pure profit: $7-10 per order with zero inventory

## Phase 10: Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks
- **AI API downtime:** Implement fallback to static templates
- **High AI costs:** Set daily spending caps, monitor usage
- **Scaling issues:** Vercel auto-scales, but monitor

### Business Risks
- **Low conversion:** Offer more free value, reduce friction
- **Competition:** Move fast, build brand loyalty
- **Churn:** Implement engagement emails, improve product

### Financial Risks
- **Negative unit economics:** Adjust pricing immediately
- **Ad spend not converting:** Pause and test new creatives
