## Commission Structure

| Tier | Commission | Requirements |
|------|------------|--------------|
| **Bronze** | 30% | Starting tier |
| **Silver** | 35% | 10+ paid referrals |
| **Gold** | 40% | 25+ paid referrals |
| **Platinum** | 45% | 50+ paid referrals |
| **Diamond** | 50% | 100+ paid referrals |

### How It Works

- You earn commission on **every payment** from your referrals
- Commissions are **recurring** for the lifetime of the subscription
- Commission is based on the plan price (after any discounts)

### Example Earnings

| Referral's Plan | Your Tier | Your Commission |
|-----------------|-----------|-----------------|
| Pro Monthly ($9.99) | Bronze (30%) | $3.00/month |
| Pro Annual ($99/year) | Gold (40%) | $39.60/year |
| Business ($29.99/mo) | Diamond (50%) | $15.00/month |

## Best Practices

### Do's

✅ Share genuine experiences with the product
✅ Target relevant audiences (event planners, brides, party hosts)
✅ Use your affiliate link consistently
✅ Disclose your affiliate relationship (FTC requirement)
✅ Create valuable content around invitation design
✅ Respond to questions from your audience

### Don'ts

❌ Spam or send unsolicited emails
❌ Make false claims about the product
❌ Use misleading advertising
❌ Bid on branded keywords (InviteGenerator, etc.)
❌ Create fake reviews
❌ Use cookie stuffing or other fraud techniques
