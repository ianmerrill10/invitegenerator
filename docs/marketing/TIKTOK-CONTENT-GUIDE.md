### CapCut Editing Formula (5 minutes per video)

1. **Import footage** (screen recording + any face clips)
2. **Trim the fat** - cut out loading times, typing
3. **Speed ramp** - 2-4x on boring parts, normal on reveals

   - Key points throughout

6. **Add auto-captions** - Accessibility + engagement
7. **Export and post**
