## Competitive Positioning

### Unique Value Propositions
1. **Beautiful Templates** - Designer-quality, modern aesthetics
2. **Easy to Use** - No design skills needed
3. **Integrated RSVPs** - Track responses in one place
4. **Affordable** - Premium features at accessible prices
5. **AI-Powered** - Smart suggestions and customization

### Competitor Analysis
- Canva - General design tool, not event-focused
- Paperless Post - Premium pricing, limited free options
- Evite - Dated design, ad-supported
- Greenvelope - Higher price point
- Zola - Wedding-only, platform lock-in

### Positioning Statement
"InviteGenerator makes creating stunning digital invitations effortless. Our designer templates, smart RSVP tracking, and intuitive editor help you create memorable invitations in minutes, not hours."
